Activities in Jakarta, Indonesia

# Taman Mini Indonesia Indah – Jakarta Cultural Theme Park (Fee: Rp 31,000)

Taman Mini Indonesia Indah is a cultural theme park in East Jakarta that highlights the traditions and heritage of Indonesia’s provinces through traditional pavilions, clothing, dances, and regional customs. The park features a central lake with a miniature of the Indonesian archipelago, a cable car, museums, the IMAX Theater, and Tanah Airku Theatre, offering visitors a comprehensive look at Indonesia’s cultural diversity and recreational attractions.

The park showcases the daily life and traditions of 26 provinces with authentic regional architecture.
Recreational facilities include a cable car for aerial views and several museums for cultural exploration.
The central lake features a detailed miniature representation of the entire Indonesian archipelago.
Taman Mini Indonesia Indah is recognized as a leading tourist destination in Jakarta for experiencing Indonesia’s rich cultural heritage.

# Dufan Ancol – Dunia Fantasi Jakarta Theme Park (Fee: Rp 193,000)
Dunia Fantasi (Dufan) Ancol is Jakarta’s top amusement park, offering a mix of thrilling rides and family attractions within the Ancol Dreamland complex. The park features themed zones inspired by fantasy and Indonesian culture, making it ideal for families, couples, and groups seeking memorable adventures. With various rides and shows, visitors can enjoy a full day of excitement and entertainment.

Dufan is divided into four main attraction types: Children Rides, Family Rides, Water Rides, and Thrill Rides.
Popular attractions include Ontang-Anting, Doll Palace, and Dream Playground, each catering to different age groups.
The park operates daily, with hours varying between weekdays and weekends.
Visitors should wear comfortable clothing, check ride restrictions for age or height, and prepare for outdoor or water-based attractions.

# Sea World Ancol – Aquarium Admission (Rp 118,000)

Experience the wonders of Sea World Ancol, where you can explore a magical underwater world with your loved ones. Discover more than 18,000 aquatic creatures and enjoy an up-close look at the diversity of marine life in Jakarta. Watch thrilling moments as sharks display their natural hunting instincts during the live feeding show.

Sea World Ancol features a giant Antasena Tunnel aquarium holding five million liters of seawater and hundreds of marine species.
Visit the Shark Aquarium to see black tip sharks, hammerhead sharks, and catch the daily live shark feeding at 14:30 WIB.
The Interactive Aquarium lets you dip your hand in the water and interact with friendly fish, demonstrating unique physics principles.
The venue is wheelchair accessible and recommends casual wear for all visitors.

# Playtopia Adventure Senayan Park – Jakarta Indoor Playground (Fee: Rp 180,000)

Playtopia Adventure Senayan Park in Jakarta is a lively indoor playground offering children a safe and creative environment to play and explore. With zones designed for ages 1–12, kids can enjoy trampolines, ball pools, climbing structures, and obstacle courses in a space that prioritizes hygiene and safety. Parents can relax in dedicated seating areas while their children have fun in this family-friendly destination.

The playground is located within Senayan Park, providing easy access for families visiting Jakarta.
Strict cleanliness and safety standards are maintained throughout all play areas.
There is a special viewing and seating area for parents to comfortably supervise their children.
Playtopia Adventure Senayan Park is suitable for birthdays, family outings, and playdates, making it a versatile choice for group activities.

# Jakarta Aquarium Safari – Indoor Aquarium & Mermaid Show (Fee: Rp 180,000)
Discover Jakarta Aquarium Safari, Indonesia's largest indoor aquarium, where you can experience up-close encounters with 3,500 land and ocean animals. Enjoy the mesmerizing mermaid show “Pearl of the South Sea,” and explore interactive exhibits perfect for families and groups. The aquarium also offers unique educational activities and opportunities to learn from expert guides.

Participate in the Akoya pearl harvesting experience and take home a special souvenir.
Children can join creative drawing activities designed to inspire and entertain.
The aquarium is open daily from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM, making it easy to plan your visit.
Jakarta Aquarium Safari is an ideal destination for family vacations and group travelers seeking adventure and learning.

# Gondola Ancol – Cable Car Ride in Jakarta (Fee: Rp 53,000)

Enjoy a unique experience soaring 21 meters above sea level with Gondola Ancol, the first high-tech cable car in Indonesia located within Taman Impian Jaya Ancol. The gondola offers panoramic views of Jakarta Bay and connects major attractions, making it a convenient way to explore the area with friends or family.

Each of the 37 Gondola Ancol cabins can accommodate up to 6 adults for a 20-minute ride.
The cable car links Pantai Festival Station to Teater Mobil Station, passing Pantai Indah along the way.
After your gondola ride, popular destinations like Dunia Fantasi (Dufan), SeaWorld, and Atlantis Water Adventure are within easy walking distance.
Gondola Ancol is wheelchair accessible and casual attire is recommended for comfort.

# Atlantis Water Adventure Ancol – Jakarta Water Park (Rp 112,000)

Atlantis Water Adventure Ancol is a vibrant water park in Ancol Dreamland, Jakarta, inspired by ancient Mediterranean civilization. The park features adrenaline-pumping slides, relaxing pools, and attractions for all ages, making it ideal for family outings and group fun. Visitors can enjoy a day filled with excitement, from thrilling rides to dedicated kids’ areas.

The park offers popular attractions like Crazy Slide, Skybox, Poseidon Wave, Antila River, and Kiddy Pool.
Locker rentals are available to keep your belongings secure during your visit.
Swimwear is required, and towels can be rented, though bringing your own is recommended.
Some rides have height restrictions, so check before heading to specific attractions.

# KidZania Jakarta – Children’s Role-Play Theme Park (Rp 141,000)

KidZania Jakarta offers a safe and interactive mini-city where children ages 1–14 can explore over 100 immersive role-play activities. Designed to foster creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving, this edutainment theme park lets kids experience various professions in a realistic environment. Trained staff supervise all activities, ensuring both fun and safety throughout the visit.

The attraction features a kid-sized city complete with buildings, vehicles, and different establishments.
Activities are tailored to develop essential life skills and encourage social interaction among children.
KidZania Jakarta provides a secure setting where children can learn through hands-on experiences.
The park is suitable for a broad age range, allowing young visitors to discover new interests and build confidence.

# Samudra Ancol – Aquatic Shows and Marine Life (Rp 236,000)

Samudra Ancol is a vibrant aquatic theme park in Jakarta, Indonesia, offering a variety of marine attractions and interactive experiences for families and animal lovers. Visitors can enjoy impressive underwater theater performances, explore giant aquariums filled with colorful marine species, and interact with friendly sea creatures. The park provides comfortable amenities such as cafes, gift shops, and relaxation areas to ensure a pleasant visit.

Thrilling animal shows and aquatic-themed performances are held regularly at Samudra Ancol.
The park is equipped with public facilities including nursing rooms for families with children.
Visitors are advised to wear suitable clothing and sunscreen to prepare for changing weather.
Samudra Ancol operates daily, with opening hours varying between 09:00 and 17:00 depending on the day.

# Nickelodeon Playtime – Indoor Family Adventure (Fee: Rp 55,000)

Nickelodeon Playtime is the first Nickelodeon-themed play center in Southeast Asia, designed for kids aged 3-13 and families seeking interactive fun. Children can explore immersive zones that combine physical activities with digital interactives, featuring beloved characters like PAW Patrol and SpongeBob. The venue offers a family-friendly atmosphere with imaginative play experiences and plenty of photo spots.

The play center features zones dedicated to PAW Patrol, SpongeBob, and other Nickelodeon favorites.
Socks are usually mandatory inside the play areas, and outside food and drinks may not be allowed.
Safety rules must be followed, and staff instructions are provided throughout the experience.
Nickelodeon Playtime is open daily from 10:00 to 22:00, offering flexible hours for visitors.

# Taman Safari Bogor Private Day Tour from Jakarta – Indonesia (Fee: Rp 899,000)

Escape the busy city and enjoy a private day tour from Jakarta to Taman Safari Bogor, where you can encounter exotic wildlife up close from the comfort of your vehicle. Experience a relaxing break as you view lions, elephants, and giraffes, and enjoy animal shows and interactive activities perfect for families.

Take a private, all-inclusive safari tour that helps you avoid the crowds and travel comfortably from Jakarta.
Watch engaging animal performances and join interactive experiences designed for visitors of all ages.
See a variety of animals, including giraffes, in naturalistic habitats during your safari adventure.
Enjoy the convenience of round-trip transport and hotel pickup, making your Taman Safari Bogor tour hassle-free.

# Playtopia Sports Gokart Ticket – Lippo Mall Puri Racing Experience (Rp 150,000)

Experience Indonesia’s first multi-level indoor karting track at Playtopia Sports Gokart Lippo Mall Puri, where the thrill of racing meets the excitement of video games. Race through spacious levels with advanced gokarts and use power-ups like bombs, missiles, boost, and lightning to challenge your friends and claim victory. Choose your time slot and arrive 30 minutes early to secure your spot and enjoy a realistic, adrenaline-filled race.

The track is located on the 2nd floor of Lippo Mall Puri in West Jakarta, operating daily from 10:00 to 22:00.
Each session includes 7 minutes of driving and a helmet, with a minimum height requirement of 130 cm and maximum weight of 120 kg.
Racers under 17 need parental approval and must sign a waiver before participating.
Tickets are non-refundable, non-reschedulable, and will be invalid if you arrive late for your chosen slot.
